# ESILV Academic Policies
Source: https://www.esilv.fr/academics/policies

## Attendance
- Regular attendance is expected. Some courses may have minimum attendance requirements.

## Grading
- Grades are based on exams, assignments, projects, and participation.
- Retake policies vary by course and track.

## Internships and Thesis
- Internships are integral to the program.
- A final project or thesis may be required depending on the track.

## Academic Calendar
- Fall semester: September–January
- Spring semester: February–June
